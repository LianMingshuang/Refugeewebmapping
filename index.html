Project Background

Our Team consists of Isaack Nyagweso, Zhen Liu, Ahna Knudsen, and Mingshaung Lian. We are four students from ICDE302 at Clark University. We have created a sample website for our client, The International Organization for Migration (IOM).

International Organization of Migration (IOM) Mission

The following are the core mission of the IOM:

  1. to help ensure the orderly and humane management of migration.
  2. to promote international cooperation on migration issues.
  3. to assist in the search for practical solutions to migration problems.
  4. to provide humanitarian assistance to migrants in need, including refugees and internally displaced people.

Information Source - IOM website

Project Purpose

Our project is designed to facilitate decision making, internal communication, and external advocacy. It is centered around the Venezuelan refugee and migrant crisis, since this is one of IOM’s focuses.

Caveat: This is a theoretical project showing the capabilities of web mapping tools. We are not affiliated with IOM.
